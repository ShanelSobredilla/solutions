The perfect app for
busy students
Not every teen was taught financial literacy in school. Solutions will guide teens in making better decisions and get them ready for the real world.
Flexible Learning Schedule
In order to balance doing school work while learning financial literacy, solutions has a feature where learners can personalize the perfect time and day for them to use the app.
Mulitple Financial Journeys
Each journey is a financial topic. Within the journey there are lessons which are called scenarios. Each scenario has an objective to get you started and the option to let you choose how long you want your lesson.
Interactive lessons
Throughout the scenario, the video will pause multiple times in between to ask you questions to solve. You will have to decide between 2 choices - One of them being the most ideal way to solve the problem.
Participate in Challenges with other learners
Every week, you will be given new challenges to test your knowledge in financial literacy. You will earn points depending on the difficulty level. The points you earn at the end of the week determines your rank. Plus! With each scenario you take, you get bonus points too!
